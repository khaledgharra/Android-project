## 🌟 About DayCraft
DayCraft is not just another basic calendar app. It is an intelligent time-management assistant designed specifically for students balancing heavy academic workloads, strict deadlines, and daily life. 

Instead of forcing you to manually micromanage every hour of your day, DayCraft automatically identifies the hidden gaps in your schedule and pairs them with your upcoming exam preparation checklists using generative AI.

---

## ✨ Core Features

### 📅 Static & Dynamic Planning
* **Visual 24-Hour Timeline:** A gorgeous, scrollable vertical layout that maps out your day hour-by-hour.
* **Life Blocks vs. Study Windows:** Lock in unmovable personal routines (lectures, sleep, travel), and let the app highlight your true "Available Study Windows."
* **Deadline Tracking:** A centralized dashboard for tracking major exams and assignments sorted chronologically.

### 🧠 Smart & AI Integration (Coming Soon)
* **Adaptive Logic      :** Get stuck or missed a session? One-tap manually shuffles your missed tasks straight into the next available free study window today.
* **AI Academic Assistant:** Tap an exam to let AI automatically break down a massive study goal into a checklist of 3–5 small, manageable tasks.
